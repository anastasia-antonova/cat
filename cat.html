We protect community cats

Perfectly healthy cats across the country are being trapped and killed in misguided attempts to manage community cat populations. Free-roaming cats are widely misunderstood, and often fall victim to deadly human ignorance and intolerance.

In Defense of Animals is challenging the outdated "feral cat" paradigm, and encouraging communities away from trap-and-kill in favor of Trap-Neuter-Return. We support humane and effective community cat management programs that undertake life-saving trap-neuter-return, and strive to protect community cats and their outdoor homes.

HELP CATS

In Defense of Animals has long advocated for community cats and acted as allies to those who protect them. Help us protect cats!
