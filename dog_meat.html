In Defense of Animals Works to End the Dog Meat Trade

Eating dog meat might be incomprehensible to most of us. But across Asia, millions of dogs are still raised for meat and transported in conditions that are beyond inhumane. Every single year, dogs are brutally slaughtered for their meat.

How we help

Adoption

We’ve rescued hundreds of dogs from the meat trade, with our Korean partner Jindo Love Rescue, and placed them in loving homes throughout the U.S. and Canada. You can help support these efforts by donating, or adopting one of these amazing dogs!

Flight Volunteers

We’re determined to rescue as many dogs as we can from the South Korean dog meat trade with our Korean rescue partner Jindo Love Rescue. Volunteering to act as a courier is quick and easy, and Jindo Love Rescue takes care of all of the costs and paperwork involved.

Outreach and Advocacy

Our Dog Meat campaign works through political, educational, awareness and action, to eliminate the market for dog meat in South Korea, and ensure dogs all over the world are seen as loving and protected companion animals and not raised as food.
